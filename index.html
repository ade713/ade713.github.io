Sorrel Juice

  • Descripton
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

A popular Trinidad and Tobago Christmas drink. Sorrel Wiki Info

Ingredients

  • White Oak Rum - 1/2 Cup
  • Sorrel - 1 1/2 lbs
  • Water - 12 cups
  • Brown Sugar - 2 cups
  • White Sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Cinnamon - 2 sticks
  • Cloves - 10
  • Ginger - 2 tbsp

Directions

  1. Wash and clean sorrel.
  2. Add sorrel, water, cinnamon, cloves and ginger to pot.
  3. Boil for 30 minutes.
  4. Cool
  5. Strain and sweeten with sugar.
  6. Add White Oak Rum, mix and Enjoy!
